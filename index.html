Meet The Chef

Our Chef: Adrastos Kostaninas

Adrastos Kostaninas, the Executive Chef of Agora Mezze and a native of Santorini, Greece, is the grandson of Agora Mezze's founders, Anastasios and Angeleke Kostaninas. Adrastos developed a love for cooking at a young age. He would walk to his grandmother's house after school and do his homework in the kitchen, relishing every bite yia-yia (grandmother in Greek) placed on the kitchen table. Adrastos learned to cook this way, utilizing yia-yia recipes. After a few years, Adrastos decided to apply to The Ikaria's Kitchen, Greece's most prestigious culinary school. Adrastos has over 30 years of experience in the kitchen, and for him, Agora Mezze is more than just a restaurant; it is the Greek culture embedded in every ingredient used in the kitchen, and chef Adrastos still utilizes yia-yia's recipe in every plate that comes out of his kitchen.
